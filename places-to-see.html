Places to see

Budapest is one of the most spectacular and interesting cities anywhere in Europe. has plenty of sights and activities to offer for everyone.

Buda Castle

High above the river Danube, on the top of Castle Hill sits this beatuful palace complex, once the home of Hungarian Kings. Originally built in 1265 you can see out across Budapest in all directions from the Castle walls.

The Buda old town is next to the castle. The old town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
