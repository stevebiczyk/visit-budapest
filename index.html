Introduction

Budapest is one of the most picturesque and interesting cities in Europe. It offers visitors beautiful landscapes and exciting activities to try. Budapest is actually two cities, Buda and Pest, separated by the River Danube. The Buda Castle and old town sit on top of the hill on the West of the river while the new town of Pest sits on the East side. We will tell you about the best Budapest has to offer so you can make the most of your visit.

Getting Around

Experience Budapest like a local and take in more history by using public transport to get around. Hop on the underground, take the furnicular railway up to the Castle or ride the trams across some of the famous bridges of Budapest. You're rarely more than a few stops away from your next adventure.
