Introduction

Budapest is one of the most picturesque and interesting cities in Europe. It has beautiful landscapes and interesting activities to try. Budapest is actually two cities: Buda and Pest, separated by the River Danube. The Castle and old town sit on top of the hill on the West of the river and new town, rebuilt since the war on the East of the river. This guide will give you a glinpse of the best Budapest has to offer so you can make the most of your visit.

Getting Around

Experience Budapest like a local and take in more history by using public transport to get around. Hop on the underground, take the furnicular railway up to the Castle or ride the trams across some of the famous bridges of Budapest. You're rarely more than a few stops away from your next adventure.
