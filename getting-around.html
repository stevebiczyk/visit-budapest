Getting Around

Travel by public transport and experience life like a local!

The Funicular Railway

Not keen on walking up the big hill? Then take the Funicular railway up the hillside and step out into the grounds of Buda Castle. The two Funicular carriages are linked so when one goes up the other comes down.
