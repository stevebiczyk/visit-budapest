Where to stay

The Corinthia Hotel

Formerly called the Royal Hotel, it is one of the finest 5 star hotels in Budapest. It is located in the heart of the city, near the Hungarian Parlament and a short walk away from the Opera House and Szechenyi Baths.

Inside you'll find the Royal Spa, a luxurious spa with many facilities and number of restaurants serving a mix of Hungarian and international cuisine.

The Four Seasons Hotel

Also located in the heart of Budapest, located in art nouveau palace, this hotel dates back to the Golden Era of Budapest. The interior is full of character with it's mosaic tiles and strinking chandelier. Inside you'll also find a fantastic spa to help you recharge after a day spent exploring the city. Also, the bar is well know for its innovative cocktails.


Step outside and you'll be greeted by the views of the majestic Danube and a great view of the picturesque Buda skyline.

Mystery Hotel Budapest

This historically themed luxury hotel is located in the 6th district, in the center of Budapest. Originally built by the Freemasons, the unique interior is full of wonder and mystique. Nearby you'll find the Hungarian State Opera, St. Stephen's Basilica and many other famous sights.


Inside you'll also find spa and wellness facilites, a great restaurant serving fantastic food.
