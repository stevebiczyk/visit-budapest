Szechenyi-Thermal-Bath

Things to do

A cruise on the Danube

Choose from an hour on an open top boat or a full day cruise taking in the 7 bridges of Budapest, historical and architectural buildings along the banks. You can treat yourself to an evening tour with a cocktail or a full 3 course meal onboard one of the floating restaurants.

Relax in the thermal baths

Hungary is well known for it's thermal baths, the Széchenyi Thermal Bath being the biggest and most famous of all. Relax in the hot pools, get pampered wtih a spa treatment, or visit the Thermal Beer spa! The perfect place on a hot summers day.

Wine Tasting Experience

Taste and learn about the Hungarian wine regions. You'll hear the stories behind the wines development. An evening tasting is rounded off with Hungarian cheese and salami for the full experience! We also recommend tasting the Pálinka, fruity spirit which is traditionally drunk as an aperitif.
